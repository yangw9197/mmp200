Fried Rice

  • Recipe by: Wolf dance

  • "If there is a taste of food that you can keep in your memory, I think "fried rice" should be considered the one."

  • Production time:10-20 minutes
  • The number of meals:4 servings
Ingredients
  • 200 grams of rice
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 cup Peas
  • 1 carrot
  • salt
Directions
  1. In a saucepan bring water to a boil. Stir in rice. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add eggs, after 1 minute adds carrot, and peas, to taste. Cook 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add rice stir-fry for 1 minute. Add soy sauce and cook 1 minute more. Remove from heat. Add sesame oil and mix well.

Temaki Sushi

  • Recipe by: Marc

  • "Temaki Sushi literally means "hand rolled sushi". This recipe is from Marc!"

  • Production time:10 minutes
  • The number of meals:1-4 servings
Ingredients
  • Batch sushi rice
  • Nori
  • Tuna
  • Ikura
  • Salmon
  • Lettuce
  • Kaiware sprouts
  • Green shiso
  • Mayonnaise
Directions
  1. Place a sheet of nori with the rough-side up in front of you and add a large spoonful of sushi rice on the left half.
  2. Wet your fingers in water and then use them to spread the rice into an even layer on the left half the nori.
  3. Place your filling ingredients on the rice, with most of the filling towards the top.
  4. Roll the bottom left corner up to the middle of the top edge of the nori to form a cone.
  5. Continue rolling until the nori wraps completely around itself. Eat immediately.
